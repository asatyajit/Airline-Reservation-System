Cancellation Charges
35% Of The Basic Fare + Transaction Fees - More Than 10 Days Prior To Scheduled Departure Of The Flight.
50% Of The Basic Fare + Transaction Fees – 1 To 10 Days Prior To Scheduled Departure Of The Flight.
No Refund If The Cancellation Is Made Less Than 24 Hours Prior To The Scheduled Departure Of The Flight though taxes are refundable.
The Refund Will Be Made In The Same Mode Of Payment Used While Booking.
